WHO WE ARE

Phi Beta Lambda is a chapter of Future Business Leaders of America-Phi Beta Lambda, the largest student business organization in the nation. Emphasizing a holistic approach to mastering business principles, PBL provides members with the technical & interpersonal foundation and opportunities needed to succeed in a dynamic business world.

OPPORTUNITY

PBL provides members with education and real-world experience. In their first semester, members undergo industry-specific professional development and our Internal Curriculum, which covers recruiting skills such as networking, resume writing, and interviewing. Afterward, members can mentor newcomers or join a consulting team to work on real-world projects, gaining professional insights, impressive accomplishments, and leadership experience.

Why Us?

In addition to professional opportunities, PBL partners with companies like Google and USBank to enhance your recruiting process through office tours, speaker events, and priority resume drops, providing valuable insights and networking with company leaders. Lastly, being a member of PBL is more than being in a professional business organization; it's being part of a supportive community that feels like family. Our club fosters a vibrant social scene by hosting frequent socials where members build lifelong friendships. Whether collaborating on projects or celebrating successes, PBL ensures every member thrives personally and professionally while having fun.
